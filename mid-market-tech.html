§ 01 / The Problem

Motion. But not progress.

Your team is experimenting with AI. Pockets of power-users, scattered tool subscriptions, the occasional internal demo. None of it tied to the strategic initiatives leadership is actually accountable for.

Motion without progress, cost without ROI, no path from "playing with AI" to "AI is changing how we operate." The fix isn't more tools — it's a program.

i.Champions burning cycles on demos no one greenlights
ii.Six SaaS subscriptions, none of them governed
iii.A board that's started asking "what's our AI story?"
iv.Pilots that run, then quietly stop, with nothing carried over
§ 02 / The Approach

Four moves. In order. No skipping.

The program is opinionated about sequence. Anchor before structure, structure before execution, and the COE last — not first. Skip a step and the rest stops compounding.

01

Anchor to existing strategic initiatives.

We don't pitch AI strategy in a vacuum. Every recommendation maps to OKRs and roadmap items leadership already owns.

02

Discovery before structure, structure before execution.

Most rollouts skip the diagnosis. We map current state, opportunity, and bottlenecks before designing anything.

03

The COE is the accelerator.

A well-designed Center of Excellence turns scattered exploration into compound returns. We design yours for your org — not from a template.

04

Your team owns it, end to end.

We're the program architect and change-management coach — not a build shop, not an agency that disappears with the playbook.
