# Revenue Residency — Program Design

## The Premise

Founder-operators at $300K–$3M revenue have momentum, not a system. They're winning despite their go-to-market, not because of it. The Revenue Residency gives them 6 weeks to build the actual infrastructure underneath the revenue — while they're still running the business.

Miyagi mechanic: learning is embedded in doing. No content delivery. Founders build their own Revenue OS, week by week, with Danny's methodology as the scaffold.

---

## Program Structure

- **Format:** Cohort (6–8 founders)
- **Duration:** 6 weeks
- **Price:** $6K–$10K per founder
- **Cohort 1 Target:** Q2 2026
- **Waitlist:** `principl.ai`

## Ideal Participant

- Founder-operator (not funded, not a solopreneur hobbyist)
- $300K–$3M revenue
- Has customers and some proof — not pre-revenue
- Knows something is broken in their go-to-market, can't articulate what
- Time-constrained but willing to do the work

## Weekly Arc

| Week | Focus |
|------|-------|
| 1 | Revenue Diagnostic + ICP. Where the money actually comes from and who's paying you. |
| 2 | Positioning + Narrative. Why you win and how you say it. |
| 3 | Demand Motion. How buyers find and choose you. |
| 4 | Sales Infrastructure. Process, pipeline, conversion. |
| 5 | Retention, Expansion + Unit Economics. Revenue you already own and the math to evaluate your system. |
| 6 | OS Assembly + Handoff. Wire it together, stress test it, leave with a system. |

---

## Format Decisions

- **Delivery:** Live weekly calls, 60 minutes, fixed schedule. Async work expected between sessions. The fixed container is the product. Founders can commit to 60 min/week even when busy.
- **Facilitation:** Solo. Danny is the IP and the draw for Cohort 1. Co-facilitator is a future hire/partner decision once the format is proven.
- **Community:** No formal community at launch. Whether to build one is a question for the Cohort 1 participants — if the group has natural chemistry post-program, spin up a Slack then. Don't promise it upfront.
- **Pricing:** Flat fee. Cohort 1 at **$8K** (positioned as a discount off the $10K standard price future cohorts will pay). Sets the future price anchor, gives early participants a reason to act now.
