# principl.ai

**Revenue Infrastructure Architect platform.**

Revenue is infrastructure, not marketing. Most founder-led businesses have momentum, not a system. Danny Cox is a practitioner-academic who runs the methodology on himself first, documents it publicly, and teaches founders to build systems they understand and own.

---

## Tracks

1. **Revenue Residency** — 8-week cohort, 6–8 founder-operators ($300K–$3M revenue), $6K–$10K/founder. Founders build their own Revenue OS week by week. Cohort 1 target: Q2 2026.
2. **Experiments / Lab** — Live AI-powered business experiments (dscrme.com, future SMB automation, other PLG plays). Proof-of-concept that the methodology works.
